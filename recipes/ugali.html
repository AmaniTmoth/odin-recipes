Ugali Recipes

Descriptions

Here I am going to give the instructions on how you can cook Ugali at your home,
First I will provide ingredients then
The steps to be followed
How to serve the meal(Ugali)

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water

Steps

  1. Prepare all ingredients
  2. Prepare all utensils
  3. Pour 1/2 of water into saucepan
  4. Boil it for atleast fifteen minutes or until it is well boiled
  5. Add 1kg of flour into boiling water
  6. Take wooden spoon,then start stirring until in well cooked ready for consumption
  7. Keep the cooked Ugali in a hotpot, ready for serving
